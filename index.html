const-l-actions

In a world full of artificial light and luminous pollution, starry skies and beautiful constellation get lost in an uniform blue tint.

Try your best and recognize a constellation by it's mere shape.

You may drag stars to stick them in a position help yourself recognize the constellations, or double click on a star to reset it's position.

Resizing the page will automatically sort a new constellation.
